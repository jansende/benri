## Unit generator

```
quantity   = value unit .
unit       = system (one|prefix|user defined prefix) dimension .
dimension  = {(base|user defined base) power} .
base       = L | M | T | I | Θ | N | J | A | C | S | B | X . //See table of dimensions
prefix     = (yocto | zepto | ... | yotta) //See table of prefixes
```
